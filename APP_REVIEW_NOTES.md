# App Review Notes Template

Paste into App Store Connect -> App Review Information -> Notes.

Poly Oracle is a standalone yes/no oracle app.

How to use:
1. Enter any text question in the question field.
2. Tap "Reveal".
3. The app speaks the question and then speaks the generated answer.

Notes for review:
- No login required.
- No paywall or in-app purchases.
- No account creation.
- No user-generated sharing features.
- No data collection or third-party analytics in this build.

If audio is muted on device, speech and effects may be inaudible by design.
